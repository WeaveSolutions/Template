## VR Architecture
- **WebXR Framework**: Standards-based VR implementation for web platforms
- **Native Integration**: Direct VR SDK integration for mobile and desktop
- **Cross-Platform**: Unified VR experience across different devices
- **Render Pipeline**: Optimized stereoscopic rendering for VR displays
- **Session Management**: VR session lifecycle and state management

## WebXR Integration
- **Session Types**: Immersive VR sessions with full environmental control
- **Input Sources**: Hand tracking, controllers, and gaze-based interaction
- **Reference Spaces**: Room-scale, seated, and standing VR experiences
- **Feature Detection**: Progressive enhancement based on device capabilities
- **Fallback Support**: Graceful degradation for non-VR devices

## Device Support
- **Meta Quest**: Oculus Quest 2, Quest 3, and Quest Pro support
- **PlayStation VR**: PSVR and PSVR2 compatibility
- **HTC Vive**: Vive, Vive Pro, and Vive Focus series
- **Valve Index**: High-fidelity VR with precise tracking
- **Mobile VR**: Smartphone-based VR using gyroscope and accelerometer

## Immersive Environments
- **3D Spaces**: Fully interactive virtual environments and worlds
- **Environment Switching**: Seamless transitions between VR scenes
- **Dynamic Lighting**: Realistic lighting that responds to user presence
- **Physics Simulation**: Realistic object interactions and physics
- **Environmental Effects**: Weather, particles, and atmospheric effects

## Interaction Systems
- **Hand Tracking**: Natural hand gesture recognition and interaction
- **Controller Input**: Precise 6DOF controller support
- **Gaze Interaction**: Eye-tracking and gaze-based selection
- **Voice Commands**: Speech recognition for hands-free control
- **Gesture Recognition**: Custom gesture definitions and recognition

## Spatial Audio
- **3D Audio**: Positional audio that responds to head movement
- **Audio Occlusion**: Realistic sound blocking by virtual objects
- **Reverb Systems**: Environmental audio characteristics
- **Voice Chat**: Spatial voice communication in shared VR spaces
- **Audio Optimization**: Efficient 3D audio processing for VR

## Performance Optimization
- **90fps Rendering**: Maintain high frame rates for motion sickness prevention
- **Foveated Rendering**: Reduce rendering load outside focus areas
- **Level of Detail**: Dynamic quality adjustment based on viewing distance
- **Predictive Tracking**: Motion prediction for reduced latency
- **Thermal Management**: Prevent device overheating during extended use

## User Experience
- **Comfort Settings**: Customizable comfort options for motion sensitivity
- **Onboarding**: Guided tutorials for new VR users
- **Safety Features**: Guardian systems and play area boundaries
- **Accessibility**: Support for users with different physical abilities
- **Social Features**: Multi-user shared VR experiences

## Content Creation
- **VR Scene Builder**: Tools for creating immersive VR environments
- **Asset Pipeline**: Optimized 3D asset creation and import
- **Animation Systems**: Timeline-based animation for VR content
- **Interactive Elements**: Buttons, menus, and UI designed for VR
- **Content Templates**: Pre-built VR experiences and environments

## Accessibility
- **Motion Comfort**: Comfort settings for motion-sensitive users
- **Alternative Input**: Support for users with limited mobility
- **Visual Accessibility**: High contrast and adjustable text size
- **Audio Descriptions**: Descriptive audio for visually impaired users
- **Seated Experiences**: Full functionality from seated position

## Analytics & Monitoring
- **Usage Metrics**: Track VR session duration and user engagement
- **Performance Monitoring**: Frame rate, latency, and thermal tracking
- **User Behavior**: Analyze interaction patterns in VR environments
- **Comfort Analysis**: Monitor and improve user comfort metrics
- **Content Analytics**: Track most engaging VR content and features
